How to Get There

Choose your preferred way to reach paradise

By Air

Fly directly to Ukunda Airstrip, located just 10 minutes from Diani Beach. Enjoy a scenic 1-hour flight from Nairobi's Wilson Airport with stunning aerial views of the Kenyan coastline and the sparkling Indian Ocean.

By Road

Drive from Mombasa via the iconic Likoni Ferry, then enjoy a scenic 2-hour journey along the coastal road. Pass through lush palm groves and traditional villages as you make your way to the pristine white sands of Diani.
