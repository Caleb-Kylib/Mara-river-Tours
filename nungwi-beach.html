Nungwi Beach

Tropical Paradise

Sun, Sand, and Sea

Nungwi, located on the northern tip of Zanzibar, shines with its sparkling white sands and azure waters. Unlike other parts of the island, Nungwi beach is swimmable throughout the day, unaffected by dramatic low tides.

Snorkeling & Diving

Take a boat trip to Mnemba Atoll, a marine conservation area famous for its crystal-clear visibility, vibrant coral reefs, and playful dolphins.

Sunset Dhow Cruise

Sail into the horizon on a traditional wooden dhow. Enjoy drumming, snorkeling, and refreshing drinks as you watch the sky burn with the colors of an African sunset.

Turtle Aquarium

Visit the Mnarani Marine Turtle Conservation Pond. Feed the green turtles and learn about the community's efforts to protect these endangered ancient mariners.
