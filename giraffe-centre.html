Discover Giraffe Centre

The Giraffe Centre in Nairobi is a unique conservation center dedicated to protecting the endangered Rothschild giraffe. This family-friendly destination offers the rare opportunity to feed and interact with these gentle giants up close. The center plays a crucial role in giraffe conservation and education, making it a perfect destination for wildlife enthusiasts and families. With its elevated feeding platform, you can literally look a giraffe in the eye while feeding them pellets, creating unforgettable memories and a deeper appreciation for wildlife conservation.
