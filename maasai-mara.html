Maasai Mara National Reserve

The Crown Jewel of African Safaris

Discover the Magic of the Mara

The Maasai Mara National Reserve is Kenya’s most spectacular wildlife haven. From breathtaking landscapes and the Great Migration to authentic Maasai culture, every moment here is pure wonder.

Game Drive

Embark on thrilling drives across vast golden plains and spot Africa’s iconic Big Five. The reserve has a magnificent collection of wildlife and it is quite possible to spot not just the 'Big Five' as often mentioned widely, but also in fact the 'Big Nine', to include the Lion, Leopard, Elephant, Rhino, Buffalo, Giraffe, Zebra, Cheetah and Hippo. Experience sunrise and sunset safaris guided by expert local rangers.

The Great Wildebeest Migration

Witness one of nature’s most spectacular events as millions of wildebeest and zebras traverse the Mara in search of greener pastures — a breathtaking safari highlight.

Bush Breakfast

Dine under a canopy of stars as you enjoy traditional cuisine in a serene wild setting — a truly authentic African experience.

Bird Watching

Discover the Mara’s colorful birdlife, from the lilac-breasted roller to the African fish eagle. Over 500 bird species await your lens and curiosity.

Hippo Watching at Mara River

Watch herds of hippos wallow, yawn, and splash playfully along the iconic Mara River banks — one of nature’s great spectacles.

Bush Diner

Enjoy a freshly prepared breakfast on the open savannah after a morning drive — with breathtaking views and gentle breezes all around.
