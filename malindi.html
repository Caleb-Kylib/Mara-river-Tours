Discover Malindi

Malindi is a historic coastal town that perfectly blends Swahili culture with pristine beaches and vibrant coral reefs. Known for its laid-back atmosphere, this charming destination offers excellent snorkeling opportunities, historic Portuguese ruins, and some of the most beautiful sunsets on the Kenyan coast. Whether you're exploring the underwater world, visiting the historic Vasco da Gama pillar, or simply relaxing on the beach, Malindi promises an authentic coastal experience.
