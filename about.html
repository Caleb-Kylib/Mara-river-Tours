About Mara Tours

Discover our story, mission, and the passionate team behind your perfect travel experience

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Mara Tours began as a small family business with a simple mission: to share the beauty of the world with fellow travelers. What started as local safari tours in Kenya has grown into a global travel company that has helped over 50,000 adventurers explore the most incredible destinations on Earth.

Our founder, Sarah Mara, started the company after her own transformative journey through Africa. She realized that travel has the power to change lives, broaden perspectives, and create lasting memories. Today, we continue to uphold her vision of providing authentic, responsible, and unforgettable travel experiences.

Our Mission

To create meaningful connections between travelers and the world's most beautiful destinations while promoting sustainable tourism practices that benefit local communities and preserve natural environments for future generations.
